They will also create visual aids of the data as well as predictions for other business members a part of the company may use them to make informed decisions.

Possible Cybersecurity Issues that Could Arise in Data Science

In data science, the most used thing in the field is data. Data that attackers will want to steal for profit or for personal use. This means that Cybersecurity issues can be very prevalent as attackers could change the data to provide false predictions.

Cybersecurity Practices Within Data Science

To prevent cybersecurity breaches there are a few company wide policies and technology that can be implemented. One of which is Multi-factor Authentication which will help to protect online data, and another is physical data protection that can help secure any documents or models that have been printed or written. Lastly, encryption and VPNs are two other practices that can help secure the data sensitive in a breach.
